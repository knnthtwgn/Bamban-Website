Community Quarantine Guidelines

Social Distancing

1. Maintain a distance of at least 1 meter (3 feet) from other individuals.

2. Avoid physical contact such as shaking hands or hugging.

3. Limit gatherings to a maximum number of people as per local regulations.

4. Stay at home and only go out for essential needs.

5. Follow floor markings or signs for proper spacing in public places.

6. Avoid crowded places and maintain distance in public transportation.

Face Masks

1. Wear a mask that covers your nose and mouth in public places.

2. Ensure the mask fits snugly and avoid touching it while wearing.

3. Regularly wash reusable masks and dispose of single-use masks properly.

4. Replace masks when they become damp or soiled.

5. Avoid using masks with valves as they may not provide adequate protection.

6. Always carry spare masks for replacement, if needed.

Hygiene Practices

1. Wash hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

2. Use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol when soap is not available.

3. Avoid touching your face, especially eyes, nose, and mouth.

4. Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your elbow when coughing or sneezing.

5. Clean and disinfect frequently-touched objects and surfaces regularly.

6. Avoid sharing personal items like utensils, towels, and electronics.
